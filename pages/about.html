About

I am the friend that talks to you about NASA fun facts, keyframes of an anime, or discuss a dota hero.


Friendly intro aside, as you have seen in my passion projects and previous work, I work with a mission to bring accessible and beautiful UI to others. I love to create, build, test, learn more about users and what products bring them joy.


I like to apply UX heuristics and emotional design approach when it comes to human-facing products. I believe that the best way to understand others is through learning about their emotions, challenges with sympathy and unclouded eyes.
