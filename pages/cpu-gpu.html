Visualization of PC Builds

Executive Summary

Building a PC was a fun and nerve-wracking experience (especially when mounting that CPU onto the thermal paste). After considering parts through exhaustive lists and reading tech documents, I realized it's a real adventure from choosing the right parts to balancing budget and purpose, and if there is any <insert esoteric technical terms> problems that come with combining the parts.


Prior to the graphic cards' price race, I was determined to build a PC to play Valorant (ended up playing Overcooked) and went on several sites to choose a combination between different parts and prices. Choosing and combining computer parts can be a challenge when it comes to pricing, models and use purpose can be quite overwhelming. With my growing interest in applying UX principles to data visualization, I start to work on visualizing computer performance data, The PC build visualizer
