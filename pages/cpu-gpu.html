Visualization of PC Builds

Executive Summary

To all PC builders out there, from a novice one (like myself) to one with color-coordinated cooler fans, My goal is to help you find and compare prices and performance that best fit your needs.


Prior to the graphic cards' price race, I was determined to build a PC to play Valorant (ended up playing Overcooked) and went on several sites to choose a combination between different parts and prices. The multitude of prices, models, brands, and tech specs of hardwares that you are unfamiliar with, can be quite overwhelming. With my growing interest in applying UX principles to data visualization, I start to work on visualizing computer performance data, Choosing and combining computer parts can be a challenge when it comes to pricing, models and use purpose.
